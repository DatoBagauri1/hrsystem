About the platform

Built for practical hiring workflows, not just demo screens.

This project pairs classic Django architecture with a premium product feel, giving employers and job seekers a clean, reliable place to manage the entire application experience.

Server-rendered and maintainable

Django templates, forms, views, messages, and permissions keep the code approachable for students and strong enough for portfolio presentation.

Role-based access control

Employers can only manage their own jobs and applicants, while seekers can only access their own applications and profile actions.

Design-friendly structure

Templates are modular, semantic, and intentionally organized so future redesigns can evolve the look without destabilizing the backend.
